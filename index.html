FERRYFALL

a harbour with no coast

Open ocean in every direction. The only place you can stand is a shoal — a pale shallow where a pile still reaches the bottom. Found there, and grow outward plank by plank.

Ships come from the horizon, and the big ones need deep water. So the money is always further out than the safety: the shoal that holds your town is also the only thing breaking the sea.

Shelter and money point opposite ways. Reach as far as you dare — or build a breakwater and make your own lee.
