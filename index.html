FERRYFALL

a town that floats

Your town has no ground. In the pale shallows a thing can stand on the bed and needs nothing. Found there, and grow outward plank by plank.

But a heavy thing can't touch bottom — a tea house, a bath house, your wharf only float in the channel, and out there they must hold onto the town behind them. The channel is also the only road your ferries have.

Where boats can't go, you can stand. Where you can stand, boats can't go. Everything worth building is in their way.
